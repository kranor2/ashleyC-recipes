ice cream recipe

delicious

ingredints

  • two cups of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • 3/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 table spoon of pure vanilla extract
  • a pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. medium bowl
  2. combine heavy cream wih whole milk
  3. add granualted sugar ,vanilla extractand a pinch of salt in to same bowl
  4. whisk until sugar is fully desolved
  5. cover the bowl with plastic wrap and place in the fridge for 2 hours or until its throughly chilled
  6. prepare uice cream maker acording to the manufacturer's instructions
  7. Pour the chilled mixture in to the cream maker and chrun according to the manufactor instructions. It will take about 20 to 25 minutes
  8. Once the ice cream reaches a soft consistenncy, transfer in to container of choice while coveringsurface with wrap to prevcent ice crystals from forming
  9. Given the choice of adding fruit, chocolate chips and many more to the ice cream
  10. Enjoy delicious home made ice creeam on the peach cobbler
