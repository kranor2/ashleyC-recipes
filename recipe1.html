peach cobbler recipe

delicious

ingredints

  • one cup of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • one cup of milk
  • 1/2 butter
  • 2 cups of fresh or canned peaches sliced
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • pinch of cinnomon

Instructions

  1. preheat oven 350
  2. place the butter in a 13 inch baking dish and leave in oven to melt
  3. in mix in bowlcombine the flour1/2 cup of sugar, baking powderand salt. Mix well
  4. add milk to bowl and vanilla extract mix to dry products
  5. arrange peach slices on the batter sprinkle the remaining 1/2 cup of sugar and a pinch of cinnamon over peaches
  6. bake in the preheated oven for about 45/50 until golden brown andbatterhas risen around the peaches
